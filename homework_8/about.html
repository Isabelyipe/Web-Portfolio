Kevin Yi is a renowned lyricist in the Chinese music industry. He had been nominated for Best Lyricist in the Golden Melody Awards, and played an important role in building the successful career of many artists, including Stefanie Sun, JJ Lin, Coco Lee, Chief Zhao and Yida Huang. Kevin is now an exclusive writer at Warner Chappell Music.

Due to his Marketing background, Kevin knows how to customize lyrics for singers. He can identify what the public likes about the singer, and write lyrics that suit the artist's style and speech. These songs helped to position and reinforce the singers’ public image. In fact, a lot of Kevin's work have benefitted their singers to rise to prominence, including James Li's “Man on Wire” (走鋼索的人)、Jordan Chan's “God, Save me” (神啊 救救我), Stefanie Sun's “Encounter” (遇見), and JJ Lin's “Practice Love” (修煉愛情).

In his lyrics, Kevin also likes to illustrate scenarios in people's daily lives. Through segments of scenes that everyone finds familiar, Kevin can make the listeners connect strongly to the song, and can guide the listeners to form a full picture in their mind that's unique to themselves.
